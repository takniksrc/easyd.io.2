How EasyD Works

Get EasyD working in the following simple steps

1. Create

Open EasyD dashboard, create a screen and publish it. All you need is a google account to sign-in the EasyD dashboard. There you can use any template of your choice to create and publish a screen. Once you signed up, you can access the EasyD online platform, even remotely.

2. Connect

Connect EasyD Player to your television. Install the EasyD Player app and configure the device id in the EasyD dashboard. You need a display and a device to display your screen for your audience or even for yourself. Connect the device with your display.

3. Display

There is no 3rd step. Just enjoy the extraordinary experience powered by EasyD and do let us know how we can improve it.

EasyD Player Application

You need EasyD Player app to show your designs on the screen. Currently, Android and Fire TV apps are available but more are on their way.

Follow these steps:
1.Search EasyD Player app in your TV app store and download.
2.Install and then open it by clicking it.
3.EasyD Player will show a PIN code, note it down.
4.Go to EasyD Server and enter the PIN code from the previous step in the Add Display section.
5.Once saved, the display will show your selected screen, automatically.
