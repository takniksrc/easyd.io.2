EasyD Privacy Policy

Updated 11 March 2023

Easy Display provides this Privacy Policy to inform users of our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personally identifiable information received from users of this website, located at https://easyd.io "the Website" and EasyD application “the App”, located at https://app.easyd.io and collectively Website and App, “the Service”. In addition to that the Service may involve EasyD Player application “Player App” for displaying content on screens.

Easy Display allows registered users to create and design screens that are published to the physical displays in their businesses using EasyD Player App or on the internet.

This Privacy Policy describes how and when Easy Display collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our services. When using any of our services you consent to the collection, transfer, storage, disclosure, and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

GDPR COMPLIANCE

EasyD fully complies with General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). All our service providers are also GDPR compliant.

HOW WE COLLECT INFORMATION

We collect information in the following ways:

  • We collect information that you voluntarily provide to become a registered user of our service (e.g. name, email address, and password). You may however visit our site anonymously.
  • We collect information from third-party social applications (e.g. Google, Facebook, etc.) when you use their sign-in feature to create an account on our service.  
  • When you upload a video or picture, we collect information about the video/picture for statistical purposes e.g. file size, file format, file name, the title of your content, etc.
  • When you browse our website or use the app, we collect information about how you use our Service (See “Log Data,” below.)  

WHERE WE STORE YOUR INFORMATION

We store your personal information on our cloud servers located in the US and European Union.

Personally identifiable information may include but is not limited to

  • First name and last name
  • Address, State, Province, ZIP/Postal code, City
  • Cookies and Usage Data

We use your Personal Information to provide the Services to you. For example

Communications: We use your Personal Information to contact you with EasyD newsletters, marketing or promotional materials, and other information that may be of interest to you. (See “Your choices regarding information,” below.)

Access tokens:  When you want to register to our service using a a supported third-party platform, we ask you to authorize EasyD to verify your sign-in and collect some personal data like name and email address, etc. When you grant us authorization we save an access token that is linked to your account on that third-party platform. We do not have access to your password or other sensitive account information which you have not given us permission for, from any of the supported platform.

Log Data:  When you visit the Service, whether as a registered User or a non-registered user just browsing, our servers automatically record information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website (“Log Data”). This Log Data may include information such as your IP address, the operating system type, browser type, browser language, the webpage you were visiting before you came to our Service, the Country, and state where you are accessing our webpage from, last time you visited our website, pages of our Service that you visit, the time spent on those pages, information you search for on our Service, access times and dates, your subscription plan if you are a paid user and other statistics. We use this information to monitor and analyze the use of the Service and the Service’s technical administration, to increase our Service’s functionality and user-friendliness, and to better tailor our Service to our visitors’ needs. For example, some of this information is visible to our support agents and they can quickly help you without asking for basic details about your browser and system, etc. We also use this information to verify that visitors to the Service meet the criteria required to process their requests.

Content:  Accountholders will upload content such as videos, text (e.g. as description, the title of screens, etc.), and pictures (e.g. profile or product pictures, etc.). This content is necessary to create and design your screens for displaying to the destination PLAYERS and to accurately display your historical data in your EasyD dashboard. All your uploaded content is saved securely on our servers inside our cloud provider infrastructure in the US and European Union. We do not, edit, modify, share or scan your uploaded content in anyway.

  • Cookies:  Like many online services, EasyD uses “cookies” and similar tracking technologies on our Service to collect information. A cookie is a small data file that is stored in your browser. We utilize cookies to enable certain features of the Service, to better understand how you interact with the Service, for marketing, and to monitor aggregate usage by EasyD Users and web traffic routing on the Service. If you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use all portions of the Service or all functionality of the Service. (See “Your choices regarding information,” below.)  
    For more information about cookies and what information they collect, see our     cookie policy and cookie declaration.
  • Analytics and History:  When you create and design screens using our Service we automatically record information that we can use to provide you with different statistics, to make sure you comply with the up-to-date marketing content requirements and keep track of your content history so that it is visible in EasyD dashboard. Information collection includes, but is not limited to, video and image size, format, title, date and time when screen was displayed, content length, name of player app where screen is displayed. We also keep a history of all your screen publishing activity. We keep the link of your videos on the third-party platforms where it is hosted (for analytics purpose). We only keep the link of your videos on the platforms where it is hosted (for analytics purpose) but not the actual video. For example if you upload your video to YouTube, we store a URL of your video on YouTube. The privacy settings of the video you have streamed apply to us, just as they apply to any other normal internet user. If you upload your video directly to our Service then in that case the video is stored on our cloud service provider servers and is publicly available just like any content on your website.

  • Information Security:  We take precautions to protect your information. When you submit sensitive information via the website, your information is protected both online and offline. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job (for example, billing or customer service) are granted access to personally identifiable information. While we use commercially reasonable safeguards to preserve the integrity and security of information collected and maintained through the Service, we cannot ensure or warrant the security of any information you provide to us or guarantee that information on the Service may not be accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed by unauthorized persons.

We do not request your credit card information, login information, or national identification numbers in a non-secure or unsolicited e-mail or telephone communication.
  • Breach Notification:  We will make any legally required disclosures of any breach of the security, confidentiality, or integrity of stored “personal data” (as defined in applicable law) to you via email or conspicuous posting on this Service in the most expedient time possible and without unreasonable delay, insofar as consistent with (i) the legitimate needs of law enforcement or (ii)  any measures necessary to determine the scope of the breach and restore the reasonable integrity of the data system.
